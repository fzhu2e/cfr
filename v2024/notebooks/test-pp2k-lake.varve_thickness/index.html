    Producing pseudo-lake.varve_thickness¶

    In this section, we illustrate how to generate pseudoproxies for lake.varve_thickness.

    Required data to complete this tutorial:

    • The PAGES2k database: pages2k_dataset.nc
    • iCESM simulated air surface temperature: tas_sfc_Amon_iCESM_past1000historical_085001-200512.nc
